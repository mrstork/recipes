layout: post
title: "Hash Browns"
date: 2000-01-01
---

- 2 potatoes
- 1 small onion
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup of flour
- Salt to taste

1. Grate the potatoes, wash them until the water runs clear. Drain well.
2. Add the egg and onions, mix well. Add the flour, stir to combine.
3. Fry until brown and crispy on both sides. Season and serve.
