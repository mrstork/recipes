- 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 cup warm water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups bread flour
- 1/4 cup olive oil

1. Prepare yeast with warm water and honey
2. Add flour and mix for 10 minutes
3. First rise ~1 hour
