- 1 1/2 cups wheat bran
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1/3 cup oil
- 1 egg
- 2/3 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 cup raisins

1. Combine wet ingredients and bran, let sit to let bran absorb liquid
2. Sift in dry ingredients and combine
2. 375F for 15 minutes
